About the Study

This comprehensive research explores how young individuals in Tirana, Albania engage with technology and creative communities. The study examines learning experiences, social connections, and participation patterns in spaces that increasingly shape professional development, identity, and innovation.

Key Findings

  • 84% of respondents cited "gaining new knowledge" as their primary motivation for attending events
  • 75% strongly prefer in-person events over online formats (43%)
  • Instagram (72%) and word-of-mouth (65.5%) are the primary channels for event discovery
  • Only 18% are frequent event attendees (monthly or more)
  • Main challenges: engagement quality, overall organization, and content depth
  • 66% of participants feel curious when attending events
